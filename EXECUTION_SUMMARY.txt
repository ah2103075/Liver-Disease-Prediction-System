## WHY VOTING ENSEMBLE?

Single Algorithm Problems:
  ├─ KNN: 77.59% - May miss some cases
  ├─ RF: 75.86% - Different error patterns
  └─ SVM: 71.55% - Unique blind spots

Voting Ensemble Benefits:
  ├─ Combines strengths of all three
  ├─ Reduces individual algorithm errors
  ├─ Higher recall (98.80%) - Catches disease
  ├─ More robust predictions
  └─ Confidence indication for each result
